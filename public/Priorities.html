How Each Metric Affects SPS

SPS combines four normalized metrics to decide what you should study first.

  • Urgency (35%): How close the exam date is. Needed so near-term deadlines move up in priority.
  • Knowledge Gap (30%): Based on confidence (lower confidence = higher gap). Needed to target weak areas.
  • Importance (20%): User-rated impact/value of the topic. Needed so high-impact topics get more attention.
  • Forgetting Risk (15%): Based on days since last review. Needed to prevent decay on older material.

Recommended study minutes are then split across topics by each topic's SPS share.
